About Me

I currently work in Institute of Network and Intelligent System, School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. I recently received my Ph.D. degree in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, advised by Prof. Zhang Xiong.  I have been studying at Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL), MIT, as a visiting student where I am advised by Prof. Berthold Horn, from September, 2015 to September 2016.

My research is on computer vision and image processing, particularly I am interested in Light Field Image Processing, including depth estimation, saliency detection, image segmentation and super-resolution.

I am looking for potential undergraduate and graduate students. Please contact me if you are interested.
