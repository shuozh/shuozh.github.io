About Me

 

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, advised by Prof. Zhang Xiong. 

 

I have been studying at Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL), MIT, as a visiting student where I am advised by Prof. Berthold Horn, from September, 2015 to September 2016.

 

My research is on computer vision and image processing, particularly I am interested in Light Field Image Processing, including depth estimation, saliency detection, image segmentation and super-resolution.
